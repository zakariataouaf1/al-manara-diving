Our Story

Born from a love
of the deep blue

Al Manara Diving was founded by two passionate, certified divers with a shared dream: to share the breathtaking underwater world of Al-Hoceima with those who dare to explore it. Nestled on Morocco's Mediterranean coastline, we offer an intimate, personal diving experience unlike any other.

Our name — Al Manara, meaning "the lighthouse" in Arabic — reflects our mission: to guide you safely through uncharted waters, illuminate what lies beneath, and bring you home changed by what you've seen.

Al-Hoceima, Morocco

Find Us

Al-Hoceima,
Morocco

Located on Morocco's stunning northern coastline, Al-Hoceima is one of the Mediterranean's best-kept secrets — turquoise waters, dramatic cliffs, and a rich marine ecosystem waiting to be explored.

Mediterranean Sea, Northern Morocco
Al-Hoceima National Park nearby
Water temperature: 18–26°C year-round
Best diving season: April – October
